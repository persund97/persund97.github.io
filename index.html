Happy Pizza Friends

Ädelfors Folkhögskola
2021 - 2021

I worked as a programmer and designer on this three-day project.

The protagonist, a cute scooter bear, is stuck inside a pizza world, and the player's objective is to catch falling pizza slices to keep the bear alive.

I contributed to nearly every aspect of the game. I created a pizza slice spawner with an object pool and randomized falling speed, positioned above the viewport. I also implemented a system where the bear grows and shrinks based on the number of pizzas collected. The pizzas at the bottom are simple 2D sprites that change according to an integer value storing the number of collected pizzas. Lastly, I animated the humorous organs that fly out when the bear explodes and created the image that appears at the bottom right and the credits screen.

Cyber Claus

Ädelfors Folkhögskola
2020 - 2020

I worked as a game programmer and UI designer on this five-day project.

In this 2D platformer game, the player avoids traps and viruses in a cyber-themed environment to reach the goal at the end of the map. I developed the character controls and shooting mechanics. The shooting mechanic is a simple object pool that sets a force and quantity for the snowballs. The gift bombs activate on key press, count down, and explode to kill nearby enemies. I used Rigidbody to create the character's movement and implemented rotation for the character when pressing the A and D keys. The arm also rotates towards the player's aim. I created the gingerbread hearts (the player's life) at the top right, adjusting the life count based on a value.

Noble's Forest Dump

Ädelfors Folkhögskola
2020 - 2020

I worked as a game programmer and designer on this five-week project.

In this 2D game, you play as a cute squirrel tasked with completing mini-games to save the world from brutal climate changes. My primary task was to create the trash bin mini-game, which is similar to Tetris. I replicated the classic game's controls, allowing the player to drop trash pieces that fall step by step within a grid. The items collide with each other, and the game ends when the trash reaches the top. I also developed the score system displayed at the top of the screen and the acorns that appear after each game over, with the sprite's fill amount increasing based on the score. This was my first experience with data storage, and I wrote a function to save the high score to a local file.
